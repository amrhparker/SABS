Our Story

At Salonix Sdn Bhd, beauty is more than a service, it's an experience. A space where everyone can feel confident, cared for and truly seen, Salonix was built on the belief that beauty should never be rushed or compromised.

Philosophy

Beauty Cosmetic
We choose only trusted, high-quality salon brands to ensure your hair receives the best care.
Cruelty-Free
We use brands that do not test on animals, beauty should always be kind.
Professional products
All tools and products we use are industry-approved and selected for performance and safety.
Non-Toxic Formula
Whenever possible, we avoid harsh formulas and prioritize gentler options for your hair and scalp.
Personalized Experience
Every service is tailored to your hair type, preferences and lifestyle.
We Love What We Do
We truly love what we do and it shows in every cut, color and treatment.

Service Menu

We enhance individial beauty with state-of-the-art technologies and personalized service. Our professional team is ready to provide the greatest service you've ever had.

Wash-Cut

Fresh cuts, gentle wash, effortless styling, all in one service. Enjoy a relaxing hair wash followed by a beautifully crafted haircut tailored to your style.

Basic Wash + Cut
RM 35
A refreshing hair wash followed by a clean, simple haircut. Perfect for anyone who wants a neat look without styling extras.
Wash + Style Cut
RM 45 - 55
A wash and advanced haircut service that includes popular styles such as:
Layer Cut – adds natural movement and volume
Steps Cut – creates cascading step layers for a sharper look
Korean Cut – soft, trendy shapes inspired by Korean hairstyles
Kids Wash-Cut
RM 25
A gentle wash and haircut service specially for children aged 10 and below. Comfortable, quick, and kid-friendly!

Dry-Cut

Quick, precise, and stylish, no washing needed. Perfect for touch-ups, shape maintenance, or when you only want a clean fresh look.

Basic & Style Dry-Cut
RM 25
A precise dry haircut designed to refresh your look, refine your shape, and enhance your natural style.
Fringe Cut
RM 10
A quick and clean bangs/fringe trim.
Trim & Shape
RM 20
Light trimming to remove split ends and keep your hair healthy.
